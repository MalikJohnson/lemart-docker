About Le Mart

A people-first, tech-powered retailer dedicated to helping you save money and live better.

Since our founding, Le Mart has grown from a single store to thousands of locations across the U.S. and beyond. No matter where you shop with us, you’ll find the same commitment to value, quality, and convenience.

We’re on a mission to meet you wherever you are—online, in-store, or on-the-go—with the products you need at prices you’ll love. While retail is changing fast, our promise to deliver exceptional service and savings remains constant.

Our Commitments

  • We empower our team to grow, thrive, and make a difference.
  • We innovate to create seamless shopping experiences.
  • We deliver unbeatable prices, every day.
  • We support healthier lives, stronger communities, and a sustainable future.

In a rapidly changing world, we evolve while staying true to our core values. Together with our customers and associates, we’re building a brighter future—one great deal at a time.
